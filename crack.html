What is Password Cracking?

Password cracking is the process of attempting unauthorized access by deciphering passwords. This involves systematically trying various combinations of characters using methods like brute-force attacks, dictionary attacks, and more advanced techniques. The aim is to exploit weak or easily guessable passwords, underscoring the need for robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

What is rockyou.txt

RockYou.txt is a widely used password list derived from a 2009 data breach. It contains commonly used passwords and is employed in password cracking for brute-force or dictionary attacks. Hackers often use it to check against user accounts due to its extensive collection of easily guessable passwords, emphasizing the importance of avoiding common choices for enhanced digital security.
